    <h1>Comments and Special Characters in HTML</h1>
    <p>HTML entities are used to display special characters.</p>

    <ul>
        <li>&amp; - The ampersand character</li>
        <li>&lt; - The less-than sign</li>
        <li>&gt; - The greater-than sign</li>
        <li>&copy; - The copyright symbol</li>
        <li>&reg; - The registered trademark symbol</li>
        <li>&trade; - The trademark symbol</li>
    </ul>
